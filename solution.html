Solutions for Youth Challenges

Young people can overcome the challenges they face with proper guidance, support, and action. Below are practical solutions to unemployment, drug abuse, and mental health problems.

Unemployment Solutions

  • Skill training and vocational programs
  • Youth entrepreneurship initiatives
  • Job creation programs and internships

Drug Abuse Solutions

  • Drug awareness education
  • Counselling and rehabilitation programs
  • Community support groups and mentorship

Mental Health Solutions

  • Access to counselling and therapy
  • Stress management and wellness programs
  • Supportive community and peer networks
