Challenges Facing Young People

Young people today face many problems that affect their growth, education, and future opportunities. Below are three major challenges affecting youth today.

Unemployment

Many young people struggle to find jobs after finishing school.

Youth unemployment can lead to poverty, frustration, and lack of opportunities for growth. Many young people lack skills or access to job opportunities.

Drug & Substance Abuse

Drug abuse is becoming a serious problem among young people.

Drugs can damage physical and mental health. Peer pressure, stress, and curiosity often lead young people to substance abuse.

Mental Health Problems

Many youths experience stress, depression, and anxiety.

Mental health challenges can affect relationships, education, and daily life. Young people need support and awareness to manage their mental wellbeing.
