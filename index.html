Welcome

Young people today face many serious challenges that affect their future. This website explains three major problems affecting youth today: unemployment, drug and substance abuse, and mental health issues. Our goal is to create awareness and encourage solutions.

Unemployment

Many young people struggle to find jobs after finishing school.

Drug & Substance Abuse

Drug abuse is a growing problem affecting youth health and future.

Mental Health

Stress, depression and anxiety are affecting many young people today.
